Getting Started with HeartBond: Heartrate Graphing on iOS and macOS

From your iOS device with Health for Heartrate Graphing

Download days of data by allowing access to Health for heartrate graphing.

Your Heartrate Data will Automatically Sync using iCloud

Your heartrate data seamlessly syncs across all your devices running the app through iCloud.

For the best synchronization experience, we recommend keeping the apps open during this process.

Please note that, due to factors beyond our control, synchronization might occasionally seem unresponsive but then suddenly resolve itself. VPN usage can affect syncing. We appreciate your patience and understanding in these moments.
